SIP vs Lumpsum Comparison

What is SIP?

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a method of investing a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds. It helps in:

  • Rupee cost averaging
  • Disciplined investing
  • Power of compounding
  • Lower investment pressure

What is Lumpsum?

Lumpsum investment is investing a large amount at once in mutual funds. It's suitable when:

  • You have a large amount to invest
  • Market is at a low point
  • You want to maximize returns in a short period
