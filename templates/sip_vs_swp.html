SIP vs SWP Comparison

What is SIP?

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a method of investing a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds. Benefits include:

  • Regular investment discipline
  • Rupee cost averaging
  • Long-term wealth creation
  • Power of compounding

What is SWP?

Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) is a method of withdrawing a fixed amount regularly from mutual funds. It's useful for:

  • Regular income generation
  • Retirement planning
  • Tax-efficient withdrawals
  • Maintaining investment corpus
